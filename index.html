How to Combat Climate Change

The purpose of this Website is to inform people about the change that is happening to our earth and what we can do to slow this change. This is our world,our future and we should care for it as we care for our body. Using this Website, I hope to inspire you, so you have the courage to talk about climate change and how it affects our world today.

Causes of Climate Change

  • Human expansion of the “greenhouse effect”, the atmosphere traps heat radiating from earth toward space.
  • The earth works the same way as greenhouses do, the sunlight gets trapped inside of the glass container, this is how the greenhouse can keep plants warm even throughout the drastic climate changes.
  • The most common greenhouse gasses are: Water vapor, Carbon dioxide, Methane, Nitrous Oxide, and Chlorofluorocarbons.
  • Over these past centuries we have been burning fossil fuels, such as oil and coal. The burning of fossil fuels has increased the concentration of carbon dioxide.

Facts about Climate Change:

  • The concentration of carbon dioxide as of May has been the highest concentration in human history.
  • 2019 was the second warmest year on record.
  • Eleven percent of all global greenhouse gas emissions caused by humans are due to deforestation.
  • The Greenland and Antarctic ice sheets have decreased in mass, losing an average of 279 tons of ice per year between 1993 and 2019, meanwhile Antarctica lost 148 billions tons of ice per year.
  • Glaciers are retreating almost everywhere around the world.
  • The amount of spring snow cover in the Northern Hemisphere has decreased over the past five decades, and snow is melting much earlier.
  • Global sea level rose to about 8 inches in the last century.
  • High temperature events in the United States have been increasing, while events of low temperatures have been decreasing
  • The ocean has absorbed between 20-30% of total carbon dioxide in recent years.
